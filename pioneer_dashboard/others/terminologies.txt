🟢 SKU

Stock Keeping Unit
→ Unique identifier for a product
Example: Adhesive A 1L = 1 SKU
Adhesive A 5L = another SKU

This is product-level tracking.

🟢 SO

Sales Order
→ Customer order placed
This is demand.

If SO exists but no stock → you must produce.

🟢 SI

Sales Invoice
→ Order already delivered and billed
Meaning: revenue recognized.

🟢 OOS

Out Of Stock
→ No available inventory

This is critical for dashboard alerts.

🟢 BACK ORDER

Order that cannot be fulfilled yet because:

No stock

Not yet produced

This is high priority.

🟢 FC

Forecast
→ Expected future demand
Usually from planning team.

Helps production planning.

🟢 RMPM

Raw Material / Packaging Material

Very important in manufacturing.

If RMPM is insufficient:
→ You cannot produce finished goods.

This connects Engineering + Procurement.

🟢 PCI

In manufacturing systems, PCI often means:

Production Control Inventory
OR

Plant Capacity Indicator
OR

Production Completion Indicator
